Sarah Klein - Bassist

Sarah Klein is a multi-instrumentalist, but prefers to be behind an upright bass.

She studied Music Theory at the University of Florida under Kevin Casseday. During that time, she performed in the Symphony Orchestra, Wind Symphony, fall musicals, and anywhere else with a need for some low notes. She also performed with the Ocala Symphony Orchestra and could be found at many brunches and bars with The Tramp Collection.

After Gainesville, Sarah moved to Seattle, WA where she performed with the Seattle Philharmonic Orchestra and the opera/bolero ensemble Encanto.

Since moving to NYC, Sarah has performed symphonic repertoire with the New York Repertory Orchestra and the Brooklyn Conservatory Orchestra, jammed some country chords on a Red Hook barge in celebration of the Schooner Apollonia, and spent a wonderful evening performing Annie Bacon's The Folk Opera, to Beacon's finest fireflies. She can be found at any number of jazz and bluegrass jams around Brooklyn.
